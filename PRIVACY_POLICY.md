# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

## Overview

Rattil is a free, offline-first Quran reading app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Rattil handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Rattil does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The app does not require an account, login, or any form of user identification.

## Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

- Reading progress and bookmarks
- Memorization (Hifz) tracking
- Daily reading goals (Wird) settings
- Tikrar session history
- Language and theme preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

Rattil connects to the following third-party services solely to fetch Quran content:

- **api.quran.com** — Quran commentary (Tafseer) content
- **quranapi.pages.dev** — Quran audio recitation

These connections are read-only. **No user data is sent to these services.** Only content requests (surah/verse identifiers) are transmitted to retrieve the requested commentary or audio.

## Analytics and Tracking

Rattil does not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry services. There are no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## Advertising

Rattil contains no advertisements.

## Children's Privacy

Rattil does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
